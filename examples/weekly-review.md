# Example: Weekly Review

## Prompt

```text
Use community-ops-weekly-review to review this week:

- one activity was launched and received 18 submissions from 9 people;
- 3 submissions are being reviewed for a showcase;
- two newcomers asked where to post their first work;
- a paid member reported an access issue that is still unresolved;
- no reliable renewal or revenue data is available.
```

## Expected Shape

- separate confirmed actions from open follow-ups;
- connect the newcomer questions to an onboarding friction;
- route the access issue to support risk and mark it unresolved;
- describe showcase candidates as pending review;
- state that renewal and revenue conclusions cannot be made;
- produce evidence-based next-week priorities.
